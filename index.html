Bell Tower

The bell tower is at the middle of the school and rings its bell every hour. It is very tall and has an interesting design. When I first came to campus it was a nice reference point for whenever I got lost. It is probably the thing that UCR is most known for.

Orbach Library

Obarch Library is a place for students to study at UCR. I like it because of its reservable study rooms and access to computers. The first floor is open for talking, and the second floor is quiet, so you can go futher up if you are studying alone. Also, it is open 24hrs on finals week, so it is my go to for late night studying before the quarter ends.
